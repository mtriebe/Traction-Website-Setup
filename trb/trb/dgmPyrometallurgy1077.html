Model: Pyrometallurgy


Description

Notes:

Definitions:

Pyrometallurgy - a branch of extractive metallurgy that involves using high temperatures to extract and purify metals from ores or waste streams through processes such as roasting, smelting, and refining. Adapted from Zhang et al. (2022) (see references below).
